Contributors & Sponsors

Our group owes its early organizational support to its association with the award-winning VSNS-BCD BioComputing Courses; some students of the 1996 course (Chris Dagdigian, Richard Resnick, Lew Gramer, Alessandro Guffanti, and others) have contributed code and commentary. Georg Fuellen, the VSNS-BCD chief organizer is one of the driving forces behind bioperl.

Uni-bielefeld provides us with our Mailing lists

Jon Orwant at The Perl Journal gave us permission to reprint Lincoln Stein's article. He also worked with the Perl Institute to arrange our perl.org DNS entry.

The initial server hardware for bio.perl.org was donated by Genetics Institute with hosting and internet connectivity (ISDN) provided by Chris Dagdigian.

In July of 1999, bio.perl.org was migrated onto a 600Mhz Alpha system donated by Compaq Computer Corporation. Server hosting facilities and internet connectivity are now provided by Genetics Institute.

We would like to thank Nat Goodman, the Compaq High Performance Technical Computing group and the Compaq Bioinformatics Solutions Center for making the hardware donation possible.
