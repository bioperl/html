Proposals & abstract submissions:

We are looking for people interested in presenting short (~20 minute) talks or posters at the workshop. Participation is by no means limited to people associated with the bioperl project, anyone doing interesting work with perl is invited to share their progress with us. Abstracts should be less than 500 words in length and can be considered for both verbal and poster presentation.


The deadline for abstract submissions is June 1, 1999.

Submissions and questions regarding presentors should be addressed to Ewan Birney at birney@sanger.ac.uk. Abstracts should be 500 words or less and emailed as ASCII text (no attachments please). You will need to apply for registration separately.
