bioperl mailing lists

Announcement list: bioperl-announce-l@bioperl.org

  • Low volume moderated list, providing announcements of major bioperl releases, upgrades & events
  • List information page at http://bioperl.org/mailman/listinfo/bioperl-announce-l
  • Web archive

Discussion list: bioperl-l@bioperl.org

  • Unmoderated list for general discussion of bioperl modules, current & future efforts and related topics.
  • List information page at http://bioperl.org/mailman/listinfo/bioperl-l
  • Web archive

Technical list: bioperl-guts-l@bioperl.org

  • Unmoderated list for the Bioperl developers and interested parties. Primarily of interest for people who want to see the bug reports that come through the jitterbug reporting system.
  • List information page at http://bioperl.org/mailman/listinfo/bioperl-guts-l
  • Web archive
