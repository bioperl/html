Users, Contributors and Participants
Core group

Bioperl is run by a core group of people from around the world. The core group is very open (most of us joined after the start of the project), and people who contribute naturally become part of the core group. You can follow developer discussions on the guts list which is where decisions about bioperl get thrashed out. Decisions are made by informal consensus. Your input is very welcome in any discussion. The only person different from any others is Ewan Birney who officially releases the core code. The core group are (currently)

  • Ewan Birney Current coordinator, Design, XS compiled code, Bio::SeqIO::*
  • Steve Chervitz. Initial coordinator, early design and Bio::Tools::Blast, Bio::Root::*
  • Chris Dagdigian System administrator, Webmaster, lightweight analysis
Previous people involved heavily in bioperl are Steve Brenner, Aaron Mackey and Georg Fuellen who are still active in discussions.

Many other people, most of them on this list have contributed invaluable code, comments, design advice and support.
