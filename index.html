About the bioperl project
Bioperl is an association of developers of public domain Perl tools for computational molecular biology.

bio.perl.org provides an online resource for modules, scripts, and web links for developers of Perl-based software for life science research.

Script Central

Help us prevent people from wasting time reinventing the wheel. Many of the tasks that perl is used for within computational biology & bioinformatics are common throughout academia and industry. In addition there are many biologists who would like to harness the power and flexibility of perl without having to become an expert programmer (or hiring dedicated computer scientsts).

We are looking to archive, mirror or provide pointers to any and all biology-related perl code that is freely available for download. If you have developed a utility or module that you think may be useful to others, please consider placing it in the public domain on your site or ours. Help advance our field!
