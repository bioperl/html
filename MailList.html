bioperl mailing lists

Announcement list: vsns-bcd-perl-announce@lists.uni-bielefeld.de

  • Low volume moderated list, providing announcements of major bioperl releases and upgrades
  • Join by sending email to majordomo@lists.uni-bielefeld.de, with no subject line, and the following message body: subscribe vsns-bcd-perl-announce
  • Web archive

Discussion list: vsns-bcd-perl@lists.uni-bielefeld.de

  • Unmoderated list for development and discussion of bioperl modules.
  • Join by sending email to majordomo@lists.uni-bielefeld.de, with no subject line, and the following message body: subscribe vsns-bcd-perl
  • Web archive

Technical list: vsns-bcd-perl-guts@lists.uni-bielefeld.de

  • Unmoderated list for the Bioperl developers. Deals with Bioperl internals, and may have high traffic.
  • Join by sending email to majordomo@lists.uni-bielefeld.de, with no subject line, and the following message body: subscribe vsns-bcd-perl-guts
  • Web archive
