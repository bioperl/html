bio.perl.org - hardware
 

Our system is attached to a small home LAN located outside the Boston, MA USA area.

We are running Redhat Linux on an old Digital i486 PC with 64MB memory and a 2GB disk. The i486 box makes a perfectly good CVS platform and HTTP/FTP/SSH server. You can see it on the right side of the bottom shelf in the following pictures. The other boxes in the picture are a Pentium clone running Win95 and the Lyris Email ListServer and a PowerComputing MacOS clone that does mail and DNS for some non-bioperl domains.

The internet connection is ISDN (56K data-over-voice) line hooked up to a Netopia PN735 ISDN router and a small NetGear ethernet hub.

Hardware donations would be nice :) Especially a SCSI DAT drive or some other backup device that can be hung off a Sillicon Graphics 02 workstation (not visible in picture).
